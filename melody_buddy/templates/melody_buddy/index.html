Finding Musicians is Difficult

Finding musicians is a difficult, time-consuming experience. Our passion drives us through the hardship only to be back at square one again in a month.

Collaborating with other musicians doesn't have to be hard, and we don't have to be limited to the town we live in or by our personal networks.

Meet Melody Buddy

Melody Buddy is a music collaboration website that unshackles our songwriting potential.

Currently in development, Melody Buddy's mission is to help musicians write better songs by building a platform to create and share original music together.

Create Music with Anyone, Anywhere

Find a musician down the street to jam with or a vocalist in Paris, France. Melody Buddy lets you spend less time searching for musicians and more time creating music. It's a win-win.

No Drummer. No Problem.

With Melody Buddy, you don't need to know a drummer, guitarist, or vocalist to have these parts in your music. When you create your song in Melody Buddy, add the contributor instrument tracks you want in the song, and a musician can upload their work to your song.

Share and Sell Your Music

When you're ready to share your music why not sell it too?

Melody Buddy's marketplace lets you sell your music and automatically share a portion of the profits with musicians who contributed to it. Whether it's your music or music you contributed to you can receive income your contributions.
