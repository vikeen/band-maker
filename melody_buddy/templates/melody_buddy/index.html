Musicians need a Community

Collaborating with other musicians doesn't have to be hard, and we don't have to be limited to the town we live in or by our personal networks.

Melody Buddy is a musician's community that unshackles your potential.

Currently in pre-launch, Melody Buddy's mission is to build a community for musicians. We want to become your source for feedback and creative inspiration.

Take your ideas and jam with anyone, anywhere

Find a musician down the street to or a vocalist in Paris, France. Melody Buddy lets you spend less time searching for musicians and more time creating music. It's a win-win.

Creative Source of Inspiration

Find musicians who inspire you. We believe that every musician brings their unique flavor to the table when it comes to music. Share you ideas and let the inspiration flow when others contribute back.
